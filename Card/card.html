Raulane Festival of Himachal

Raulane refers to an ancient, mystical winter festival in the Kinnaur district of Himachal Pradesh, India, celebrating the departure of Saunis (celestial fairy spirits) who protect villagers during harsh winters, involving masked men embodying a divine couple (the 'Raula' groom and 'Raulane' bride) performing sacred dances to welcome spring and ensure future protection
